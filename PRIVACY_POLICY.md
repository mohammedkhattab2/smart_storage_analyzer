## How Smart Storage Analyzer Works

### Local Processing Only

All features of Smart Storage Analyzer operate entirely on your device:

- **Storage Analysis**: All calculations and analysis of your device storage happen locally
- **File Categorization**: Files are categorized on your device without any external processing
- **Statistics Generation**: All statistics and charts are generated on your device
- **Data Storage**: Any preferences or settings are stored locally on your device

### No Data Transmission

Smart Storage Analyzer does not transmit any data from your device:

- No cloud services are used
- No external servers are contacted
- No information leaves your device
- All operations are performed offline

## Permissions We Request

Smart Storage Analyzer requests the following permissions to function:

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: To read storage statistics and analyze file categories
- **What we access**: File sizes, types, and folder structures
- **What we DON'T access**: File contents, personal data within files
- **When it's used**: Only when you actively use the app to analyze storage

This permission is essential for the app's core functionality and is used solely to provide you with storage analysis features.
